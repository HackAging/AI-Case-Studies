# The AI Scientist-v2 and AgentRxiv

## Description

A comprehensive multi-agent system for autonomous scientific research that combines AI Scientist-v2 (an agentic tree-search research system with automated manuscript generation) with AgentRxiv (a collaborative agent research substrate with shared agent research memory). The system enables fully autonomous research workflows from hypothesis generation to manuscript publication, with the first AI-generated workshop-accepted paper in 2025.

## Mission

Enable fully autonomous research workflows from hypothesis generation to manuscript publication, improve cross-lab knowledge reuse and collaboration, accelerate scientific progress through collaborative knowledge sharing

**Publication Date**: April 2025

- Agentic Tree-Search Research System
- Automated Manuscript Generation
- Collaborative Agent Research Substrate
- Shared Agent Research Memory
- Multi-Agent Coordination
- Vision-Language Models

## Lessons Learned

### Achievements

- First AI-generated workshop-accepted paper at ICLR 2025
- 11.4-13.7% accuracy improvement through collaborative knowledge sharing
- Fully autonomous research workflow from hypothesis to publication
- Improved cross-lab knowledge reuse and coordination
- Scalable research workflows without proportional cost increase

### Implications

- Opens new possibilities for AI-driven scientific research
- Demonstrates potential for multi-agent research systems
- Shows value of collaborative knowledge sharing in research
- Enables faster research progress through cumulative learning
- Addresses key bottlenecks in research coordination and scaling

### Challenges

- Requires verification of AI-generated results
- Need to ensure novelty of hypotheses
- Quality control and peer review challenges
- Ethical considerations for AI-generated research
- Potential for knowledge propagation errors

### Impact on Field

Demonstrates the feasibility of autonomous research systems and collaborative agent research, opening new pathways for accelerating scientific discovery through AI-driven approaches.

### AgentRxiv
**Type**: platform
**Status**: Operational
**Role in Project**: primary
**Relationship Description**: Collaborative agent research substrate with shared memory
**Description**: A collaborative agent research substrate that functions as a centralized preprint server for autonomous research agents
**Capabilities**:
- Agents can upload their research reports
- Agents can retrieve and access research reports from other agents
- Similarity-based search mechanism for finding relevant previous research
- Enables multiple agent laboratories to work in parallel while sharing knowledge
**Technical Details**:

modeling: Modeled after existing preprint servers like arXiv
